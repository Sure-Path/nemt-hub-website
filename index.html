How It Works

From call to completion

01
Member Calls In
A health plan member calls your call center requesting a ride to a medical appointment.
02
Agent Verifies & Books
The agent looks up the member, checks eligibility in real-time, selects the right vehicle, and dispatches a provider — in under 2 minutes.
03
Provider Receives Ride
The transportation company gets an instant notification with all ride details and member information. They assign a driver.
04
Member Gets Updates
Automated SMS confirmations and day-of reminders go to the member. A dispatch alert fires when the driver is en route.
05
Ride Completed
The driver marks the trip complete. The system logs it, closes the authorization, and triggers a satisfaction SMS — fully automated.
